About Me?

Hi, I'm Pranav Panchal, a mechanical engineer with a strong interest in the data industry. With my experience as a product design engineer, I have developed a deep understanding of materials and manufacturing constraints, which helps me evaluate the feasibility of designs. I'm excited about transitioning into the data industry and utilizing my technical skills to analyze and interpret large sets of structured and unstructured data.

My passion lies in the implementation of machine learning techniques, particularly those that utilize unstructured data. Additionally, I enjoy working with signal processing and extracting features from it. As an ambitious individual, I'm committed to continuously expanding my knowledge in the field of data science and applying it to optimize processes and drive business success.
